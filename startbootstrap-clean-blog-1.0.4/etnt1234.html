What are YOUR favorite foods? Do you know if they are good for you? Well here are a list of goodies that are not just healthy but delicious too!

Good Food Description Bad Food

Most store-bought granola bars contain loads of sugar, especially high fructose corn syrup, which is something you do not want to eat. Making home-made granola bars gives you control of what goes inside them. There are many ways to make granola bars at home where you can adjust how much you make and what you put inside.recipe

French fries made from white potatoes are one of the most common favorite children's food. This side dish is loaded in fats and unhealthy carbohydrates that will slow you down and make your body feel heavy. Try this alternative of....PURPLE POTATO FRIES! Purple potatoes have healthy carbs that will not slow you down. Try baking them as well, just drizzle with coconut or olive oil, leave the salt out and you will have a super yummy and cool looking side that I'm sure you'll be excited to eat it...It's PURPLE!

Spaghetti sounds so yummy especially with a ton of cheese on top, right? Well this food does not have many nutrients, it doesn't really have anything that will be good for you. How about switching to veggetti? This is just a vegetable spaghetti. Use squash instead of noodles. This one switch, will add a serving of vegetables to make you feel better and healthier, so give it a try! I promise you won't regret it!

An order of 4 chicken nuggets already has 190 calories and 400mg of sodium. Control what you eat by making your own! With you parents help, cut some chicken to pieces, season *limit the salt*, dip in eggs, and then bread crumbs. It's that easy! Just bake and it's ready to eat. Click here for therecipe.

White bread barely has any nutrition to help you grow big and strong, so next time you're craving a P&B sandwich or a grilled cheese, try whole grain bread instead. Whole grain bread has healthy carbs to help you with your day!

Fruit snacks are just sugar. Don't fall for their trap especially when they put "Made With Real Fruit", so next time you're craving fruit snacks, just go for REAL fruits. It will be sooo much better for you!

Do you love mac & cheese? Don't worry I love it too. However, it is not the healthiest thing for our bodies. It usually has a lot of salt and bad fats, especially the box kind. Make your own mac & cheese so we know what goes in it. Here is a recipe you can make with your parentsBetter mac & cheese

Juice pouches are very common, but they are one of the worst things you can drink! All types of juices like Capri Sun, apple juice,orange juice, and many more have A LOTof sugar. You are only allowed 12 grams of sugar in one day. One juice pouch has around 18 grams of sugar, so it's more than you can have. Next time you want a drink, just have water or low-fat milk, they are way better for you. Plus, the milk will give you strong bones and teeth!
